Franz Bardon exhibition in Opava



A chamber exposition FRABATO will take place in Opava to celebrate the 90 anniversary of Mr. Bardon's birthday (1.12.1909).

The Exposition with the title FRABATO is the first activity in Opava that is dedicated to Mr. Bardon.

Only several souvenirs have ramained. Nevertheless family members and organizers have gathered several interesting exhibits. On 35 m sq you will see a few personal articles, typewriter, family photographs, certificate of birth, marriage certificate, records in matrix, books in all published translation, raw publication and translation into Russian. There will be showed web pages devoted to Mr. Bardon. You can buy any book of Mr. Bardon. Exposition is going to take place in AVE CENTER (center of tourist and cultural services), which is placed in Silesian provincial museums in Opava. (In Czech language:  AVE CENTRUM (stredisko turisticko-informacnich a kulturnich sluzeb) v budove Slezskeho zemskeho muzea v Opave, Ostrozna c. 42.).

The exposition will be opene to the public from 2.December 1999 to 10.January 2000.
