The cipher alphabet

This cypher alphabet was used by Franz Bardon to communicate spirit names to his students and friends.
 
A = E F = V LH = SCH R = T V = D
B = R G = W M = L S = N W = P
CH = H I = O N = G T = B Y = J
D = M K = Z O = U TZ = K ZH = CH
E = A L = S P = F U = I Z = C

The 360 spirits of the Earth Zone

Most interesting is Mr. Stejnar's finding, that all 360 spirits of the "earth girdling zone" have been mentioned also by Abramelin the Mage as given in the German edition by Peter Hammer 1725.

They are ordered by Bardon in a different way: he gives the spirit from the first to the 30th degree of every zodiacal sign. On the other hand Abramelin gives the spirits of the first degree of every zodiacal sign, then of the 2nd degree of every zodiacal sign, then of the 3th degree and so on.

Stejnar explains that Abramelins method has some advantages because the spirits of the same degree of all zodiacal signs are somewhat related to each other. The first and second degrees of every zodiacal sign are for example "positively electric" and all spirits of these first two degrees are working with the "Electric Fluid" and are related to the development of faculties like creativity, personality and power. The 9th, 10th, 19th, 20th, 29th and 30th degrees of all zodiacal signs are related to the element of water and the "Magnetic Fluid".
